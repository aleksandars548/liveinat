Residence Registration in Austria

11 min readLast updated 2026-05-06

Residence registration is easier when you separate address registration, main or secondary residence and immigration status.

Disclaimer: This guide is informational only and not legal, tax, immigration, medical, financial or professional advice. Rules can change. Always check the official Austrian source before applying.

Newcomers often use the phrase residence registration to mean different things: registering an address, declaring a main residence, dealing with a secondary residence or proving permission to stay. These are not always the same.

This article is informational only. Rules can change. Always check the official Austrian source before applying.

1. Main residence versus secondary residence

Austria distinguishes between where you mainly live and other registered addresses. The exact meaning and consequences depend on official rules and your situation.

2. Relation to the Meldezettel

The Meldezettel is the form connected to address registration. It is not the same as a visa or residence permit.

3. Austria-wide notes

Use oesterreich.gv.at for national registration information and forms. Confirm whether your situation requires additional residence or immigration steps.

4. Vienna notes

If you live in Vienna, use the Stadt Wien pages for local instructions, office details and German wording.

5. Documents

You may need ID, address information, accommodation confirmation and other documents depending on your case. Verify the official list before attending appointments.

6. Moving or changing status

If you move, leave Austria or change your living situation, check whether an update or deregistration step applies.

Practical checklist

  • Clarify whether you are registering an address or dealing with residence status.
  • Check official Austria-wide information.
  • Check Vienna pages if living in Vienna.
  • Prepare ID and accommodation documents.
  • Keep confirmations.
  • Recheck obligations after moving.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Confusing address registration with immigration permission.
  • Following advice for a different municipality.
  • Using outdated forms.
  • Ignoring updates after moving.
  • Not saving confirmations.

FAQ

Is main residence the same as residence permit?

No. Address registration concepts are separate from immigration permission.

Where do I register in Vienna?

Use the official Stadt Wien residence registration pages for current instructions.

Do I need to update registration after moving?

You may. Check official instructions for your situation.
