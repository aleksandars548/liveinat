Editorial standards

Editorial Policy

How Live in Austria researches, writes, updates and corrects content.

Research

Articles are written in simple English and prioritize practical usefulness for newcomers. Official Austrian sources are linked when topics involve government, residence, work, tax, insurance or public services.

Safe wording

We avoid inventing legal rules, exact deadlines, benefit amounts, tax rules or official requirements. When details may change, articles remind readers to verify official sources.

Updates

Each article includes a last updated date. Content should be reviewed regularly when official processes, links or services change.

Corrections

Readers can email corrections to hello@liveinaustria.com. Helpful corrections include the page URL and an official source.

Affiliate independence

Affiliate relationships, if added, should not decide editorial conclusions. Recommendations should be useful, transparent and relevant.
